AboutUs

Uniflex is a lifestyle retailer that helps students on campus to pick out the hottest and newest trends for fashion. The Uniflex websites show cases what we have in store to allow students to look at our current collection before coming into the store to try it on. Uniflex carries a wide range of clothing and depending on the season we carry different type of items. All Uniflex clothing are hand picked so the quantity of each item is very limited.
